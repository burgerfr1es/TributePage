Dr. José P. Rizal

The National Hero of the Philippines

Here's a time line of Dr. José P. Rizal's life:

  • 1861 - Born in Calamba, Philippines
  • 1861-1870 - Informal education by his mother.
  • 1870 - Received formal education in Binan for 2 years.
  • 1872 - Execution of GOMBURZA.
  • Imprisonment of Dona Teodora.
  • Rizal entered Ateneo.
  • 1872-1877 - Rizal's Ateneo days.
  • 1877-1882 - Philosophy of letters and Medicine at UST.
  • Studied Land Surveying in Ateneo.
  • 1882 - 1st travel to Europe.
  • 1882-1885 - Licensate in Philosophy of Letters. Also went to Paris for vacation.
  • 1887 - Publication of Noli Me Tangere in Berlin.
  • 1888 - 2nd travel to Europe to annotate De Morga's work.
  • 1887-1888 - Went back to the Philippines and was involved in land dispute.
  • 1889 - Cekebration of Universal Exposition in Paris.
  • 1890 - Expulsion of farmers from Hacienda de Calamba.
  • 1891 - Publication of El Filibusterismo in Belgium.
  • 1892-1896 - Exile in Dapitan.
  • 1892 - Hongkong, to build a colony in North Borneo.
  • 1892 - Founded La Liga Filipina in Philippines.
  • 1896 - Execution of Rizal in Bagumbayan.

"I honor the father in his son, not the son in his father. Each one receives a reward or punishment for his deeds, but not for the acts of others."

-- Jose Rizal Noli Me Tangere (Touch Me Not)
